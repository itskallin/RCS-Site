What is Heroes United?

Heroes United is an amateur Heroes of the Storm league where 4 eports organizations have joined forces to create a structured system to provide gamers with weekly Heroes of the Storm tournaments! The system presents gamers with fun and safe environments to grow as individual players as well as teamplayers, granting each team the potiental to win great prizes!

How does it work?

Each of the 4 organizations will host a Heroes of the Storm event on a unique day of the week for a 6 week period and compete for a position in the top 8 teams. For the 7th week, the top 8 teams will be invited to the finale tournament to battle for the first place prize. Play in as many of the weekly tournaments as you can to gain an advantage!

Partners, Patrons, and Prizes!

The organizations forming the partnership to bring you Heroes United are RandomCS, Sweet Synergy, Divergent Gaming, and HeroesHype. Matcherino is an additional patron also sponsoring the league. Each of the partnered organizations have commited $100 each to the prize pool and the inclusion of Matcherino, the prize pool is estimated to be $500 with potential for more!
