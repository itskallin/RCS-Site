Paul
Hearthstone
Paul is known for his light hearted, yet statistical casting style. He adds satire to a cast that can possibly be somewhat stale. Paul's goal is to bring smiles to the viewers faces, because of this he has quickly risen to an extremely solid all around hearthstone caster. He is currently branching out into other games guided by hhis passion for esports. Among these games are League of Legends and Overwatch and can be found casting these with his RCS buddies. Paul has also previously competited as a profesional ESL League of Legends and Evolve player. Paul may be light hearted, but is extremely competitive and has a very solid drive to find greatness. All in all, RCS will help shape him to be one of the best!
