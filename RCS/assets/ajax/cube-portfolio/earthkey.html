Earth Key
League of Legends
EarthKey is known for his clear play by play and dynamic casting style. He joined the RCS and immediatly found success casting the Elo Legends tournaments and was voted the best play by play caster in 2015. EarthKey however did not let this stop him, he sought new challenges and began coaching a Diamond team to help polish his skills. His future may be unknown but the RCS knows fate has big things in store for him.
