Important Information Highlights

What is Heroes United?

Heroes United is an amateur Heroes of the Storm league where four esports organizations have joined forces to create a structured system to provide gamers with weekly Heroes of the Storm tournaments! Heroes United provides a support structure for weekly tournaments organizations and creates greater opportunities for Heroes of the Storm teams!

How does it work?

Each of the four organizations will host a Heroes of the Storm event on a unique day of the week for a seven week period. Based on a team's placement in the weekly tournaments, teams will earn Heroes United Points (HUP). In the eighth week, the top eight teams with the most points will be invited to the Heroes United Tournament to brawl it out for the prizes.

Partners, Patrons, and Prizes!

The organizations forming the partnership to bring you Heroes United are RandomCS, Sweet Synergy, Divergent Gaming, and HeroesHype. Matcherino is an additional patron also sponsoring the league. Each of the partnered organizations have commited $100 each to the prize pool and the inclusion of Matcherino, the prize pool is estimated to be $500 with potential for more!
